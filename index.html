About Matthew

From construction sites to corporate offices, my journey is a testament to hard work and resilience. Since moving to Lexington in 2019, my goal has been to serve and uplift our community. Unaffiliated with any party, I stand for the value in every person, aiming to make District 8 a safer, better place to live.

About District 8

District 8, a vibrant area of Lexington, boasts a diverse range of neighborhoods, each with its unique charm. The district is enriched with public spaces like Gainesway Park and Tates Creek Golf Course. It's a community built on strong traditions and a forward-thinking mindset.

My Initiatives

  • Boosting Education: Supporting our schools to ensure quality education for every child.
  • Encouraging Growth: Promoting local businesses and sustainable development.
